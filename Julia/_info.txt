The folder contains Julia code to perform simulations.

files:
- `01-simulate-data.jl` -- data simulation functions
- `02-randomization.jl` -- functions to simulate randomzation procedures
- `03-t-test.jl`        -- functions to perform population- and randomization-based t tests.
- `04-ancova.jl`        -- functions to perform population- and randomization-based ANCOVA.
- `05-mmrm.jl`          -- functions to perform population- and randomization-based MMRM.
- `06-nlmem.jl`         -- functions to perform population- and randomization-based NLMEM.
- `90-simulation.jl`    -- a main script, where simulations are perfromed.
